RISK MANAGEMENT PROJECT

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The 2018 ASC/AGC Region II Student Competition project is a 200,000 square-foot, 7-story, office building with a 3-story parking deck in the Southeastern United States.

The project team has been asked to evaluate the risks associated with taking on this high-profile project. The team will be asked to identify and explain risks associated with the construction cycle – from pre-bid to post-construction – to ensure the protection of both the GC and the Owner. The team will be asked to address a gamut of concerns that include identifying risk, early negotiation concerns, and prioritizing concerns from a GC perspective, general conditions impacts, develop risk mitigation strategies, create means to track and quantify risk, and the ability to communicate those concerns to the entire project team.
